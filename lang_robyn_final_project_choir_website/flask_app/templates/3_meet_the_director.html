Meet Our Director



Stephen Manders, OUC Minister of Music




Stephen Manders is a native of Bermuda. He has lived in America since he came to Oakwood University for school in 2004. He is a former director of Dynamic Praise, and has written numerous gospel songs for his own group, Stephen Manders & Decree, as well as for other popular gospel artists. Stephen has been the director of VOI since 2015.
